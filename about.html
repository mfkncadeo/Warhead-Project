Our History

The candy was invented in Taiwan in 1975 and was first imported to the United States in 1993 by The Foreign Candy Company of Hull, Iowa.
Impact Confections acquired the brand in 2004.
They are currently manufactured and distributed in the United States by Impact Confections and in Australia by Universal Candy.

Our Mission

To bring the most extreme sour candy experience to our customers.
